ImgReName automatically moves the selected images/videos into folders that are named according to the format yyyy-MM-dd and names each image after the time it was taken. There are two naming methods to choose from::

Original ImgRename von Michael IMG_Datum_Uhrzeit
Names the files by making the year and month a letter (2000=A ,2001=B, ...) (January=A, February=B, ...) and writing them in succession. If the day of the month is less than or equal to 9, it writes the corresponding number after it. If that is not the case, it also turns it into a letter (10=A, 11=B,...). After that it writes the result of hour * 3600 + minute * 60 + second. For videos, it also writes VID_ at the beginning of the name.
Names the files according to the format IMG_yyyyMMdd_HHmmss. For videos it writes VID at the beginning of the name instead of IMG.
